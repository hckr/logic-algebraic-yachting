Logic-algebraic yachting

The application assists in making decisions regarding sailing maneuvers.

Zobacz wersję polską.

The purpose of the analysis task is to get all possible combinations of the results of the decisions made under the assumptions of external conditions (interferences). Decisions and disruptions are entered in the form of an input fact.

The decision-making task is used to get all possible combinations of decisions that result in a set of results given in form of an output fact.

In order to enter the fact, you must first define its template, consisting of logical functions and question marks in place of variables. Sample fact templates:

  • ? and ?
  • ? or ?
  • ? xor ?
  • not ?
  • if ? then ?
  • ? and (not ?)
  • ? and ((not ?) or ?)

After you enter the logical sentence template, press the "Done" button, which will change the question marks into drop-down lists to select specific sentence components. The result will be shown after all options have been selected.
